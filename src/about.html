About Us

Marcom Towns is directly across the NCSU varsity parking lot in South Campus, minutes away from Centennial Campus. NCSU Wolf Line Station is located about 300 ft away from these properties. It is close to Downtown Raleigh, Hillsborough St, the Village District, and Meridith College.

These houses maintain a quiet atmosphere while also having the convenience of shopping and restaurants.

There are 11 total units located at 3601 Marcom Street, 3607 Marcom Street, and 1405 Maxi Place.
